Psychology Notations

  1. Piagets cognitive development - Our cognitive development activities are mapped out to cater toward each age group’s general development stage.
  2. Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs - We incorporated maslow’s hierarchy of needs in ensuring our children will receive every necessity that we can provide.
  3. Multiple Intelligences - Our activities were designed around promoting growth in several areas of intelligence.
  4. Creativity and Cognitive Program - https://naturalmath.com/about/ this website displays information about “natural math” it was developed by a Maria Droujkova, PhD mathematics education, who says that five year olds can learn calculus through playful exploration of the idea of infinity.
  5. Operant Conditioning - Operative conditioning is the sum of positive reinforcement, positive punishment, negative punishment, and negative reinforcement. The sole one which we decided to focus upon was negative punishment as the best punishment was to have them help us with the routine cleaning.
