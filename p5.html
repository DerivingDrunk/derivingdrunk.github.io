Behavior Management

Insubordination

The act of purposely not following orders from one of the adults/serperiors, includes purposely failing/not doing good enough.

Stealing

A child taking something from another child or from the school/daycare on purpose.

Fighting

Two or more children having a disagreement that devolves to needless arguing and/or rough physical contact.

Bullying

Repeated attempts to physically emotionally or mentaly harm another child. Purposely endangering a child in any serious regard

Our Plan

  1. Have a conversation with the child and let them know why what they did was wrong.
  2. give the child two options. They can sit in a time out or help clean up messes and do other chores for the day care, where they will learn about doing chores effectively.
  3. Mark the child’s behavior tracker until they complete the option
  4. Add a tally on the internal behavior tracker which will count up to 10 until a parent is notified.
