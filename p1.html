Cognitive Development Features

Sensorimotor

Show changing number of items and measure where the babies focus is. We want to simply stimulate their brains to logical comparisons. At this age there is not much else we can do. It may be constructive to have shape toys and show puppet shows involving logical comparisons like which side has higher quantity or changing quantity.

Preoperational stage

Number games: come up with the biggest numbers. Talk about infinity. Talk about paradoxes with infinity. Challenge them to come up with ideas about infinity. We will start with the hilbert hotel experiment. Next we will talk about infinitely small quantities. We will make them familiar with the idea of “approaching.”

Concrete operational

We want to start, and/or continue, discussing the idea of infinity. At this stage we can safely start adding symbols and other notation to this idea of infinity. It is productive to exercise this idea of putting definitions on ideas, as it can become incredibly powerful as an intellectual tool throughout life.

Formal operational

We will start working more intensively with the notation and expanding into more specific applications of the idea of infinity, such as calculus.
