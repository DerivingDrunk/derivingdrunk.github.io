Building & Child Care Features s

Physical Needs

Depending on the child’s age different food and drinks will be supplied to each individual. Each age group will be given different types of food drinks and snacks as to avoid any mishaps.

Safety of your child

If there are any emergencies the current caretaker/stage director (person in charge) will take certain precautions depending upon the emergency. The building also has silent alarms, fire extinguishers, and a safe room because safety is of the utmost importance.

Group belonging/no child left behind

During all the daily activities everyone within the group will have to participate in one form or another. The active will change depending on the age group and the respective mental age.
