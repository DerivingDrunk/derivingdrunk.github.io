Creativity Program

  • Creativity is important in developing a sense of identity. Through creativity, children are able to express themselves and find out who they are.
  • Our creativity is woven within our entire program. Even in mathematical thinking we seek to instill a capability for originality within the kids. We will have the kids think about original infinite paradoxes, coming up with their own examples under our mentoring, and even acting out plays, letting them add their own dramatic flair to the characters and bring them to life
